Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

The Ring App: Explore Wagner ("the app") is developed by Greg Langmead, operating as Luminous Designs. This policy describes what information the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data the app does not collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no telemetry of any kind. The app does not use cookies or fingerprinting.

Apple Music

The app uses Apple's MusicKit framework to play audio from your Apple Music subscription. MusicKit communicates directly between your device and Apple's servers. The app never sees or stores your Apple Music credentials or listening history.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. The app verifies purchase status on-device only. No purchase information is sent to any external server.

Wikipedia content

The app loads synopses and background articles from Wikipedia when you request them. These requests go directly from your device to Wikipedia's servers. The app does not proxy, cache on a server, or otherwise intermediate these requests.

On-device storage

The app stores your preferences (selected recording, playback position, and purchase state) locally on your device. This data is not transmitted anywhere.

Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children. It does not collect information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The effective date at the top will be revised.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to help_luminous@luminousdesigns.tech.
